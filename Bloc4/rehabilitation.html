Evidence-Based Approaches

🎯 Risk-Need-Responsivity Model

Tailoring rehabilitation programs based on individual risk assessments, criminogenic needs, and learning styles for maximum effectiveness.

  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Resource efficiency
  • Better outcomes

🏭 Prison Industries Programs

Work programs that provide real job experience, skills training, and income while incarcerated, with guaranteed employment upon release.

  • Real work experience
  • Income generation
  • Job placement assistance

🔄 Therapeutic Communities

Residential treatment programs where inmates live together in a structured environment focused on behavioral change and mutual support.

  • Peer support systems
  • Structured environment
  • Long-term behavioral change

📱 Technology-Enhanced Learning

Digital platforms, virtual reality training, and online education programs that provide modern, engaging rehabilitation experiences.

  • Modern learning tools
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Digital skills development

Coalition Rehabilitation Models

🇵🇭 Philippines

Therapeutic Community Modality Program

Comprehensive drug rehabilitation program using therapeutic communities, achieving 70% success rate in preventing reoffense.

🇧🇷 Brazil

APAC System

Alternative Penal Method emphasizing human dignity, responsibility, and community involvement in prisoner rehabilitation.

🇰🇪 Kenya

Open Prison System

Minimum security facilities where inmates work in the community during the day, building social integration skills.

🇳🇿 New Zealand

Māori and Pasifika Units

Culturally-responsive rehabilitation programs incorporating indigenous values, family involvement, and traditional healing practices.

🇳🇬 Nigeria

Skills Acquisition Programs

Comprehensive vocational training in agriculture, crafts, and technology with guaranteed employment partnerships.

Reentry Support Systems

🏠

Housing Assistance

Transitional housing programs and support for securing permanent housing upon release.

💼

Employment Services

Job placement assistance, resume building, and employer partnerships for formerly incarcerated individuals.

🩺

Healthcare Continuity

Ensuring continued access to mental health, addiction treatment, and medical services after release.

📋

Document Restoration

Assistance obtaining identification documents, driver's licenses, and other credentials needed for reintegration.

👥

Mentorship Programs

Peer support and professional mentorship to navigate challenges and maintain positive life changes.

⚖️

Legal Support

Assistance with parole requirements, record expungement, and navigating legal barriers to employment.

Room 4 Rehabilitation Commitments

Our coalition is developing international standards for prison rehabilitation programs, creating knowledge-sharing platforms, and establishing outcome measurement protocols to ensure effective, evidence-based rehabilitation worldwide.
